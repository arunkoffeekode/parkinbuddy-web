ParkinBuddy Capabilities

Parkinbuddy is a dynamic application that allows you to look for a parking location based on your destination or based on the nearest landmark! It has a minimalistic look and feel that allows users of all technological sensibilities to navigate through it seamlessly. You can also filter for parking bay types including disabled, limited waiting, pay & display - or anything else to suit your needs.

The application allows you to pay directly through your phone, eliminating any wait time at the location; simply enter, park and be on your way! The payment feature also saves current and previous parking sessions, meaning you can have instant access to previous parking and invoices.
